## 🎭 Performance Tips:
- **Pacing is Everything:** Practice with the slides to make sure you know exactly when to click so your words match the screen perfectly.
- **The "SAME" Drop:** On Slide 26, intentionally pause right before "SAME." It's the punchline of the whole presentation!
- **Eye Contact:** During the "What will you learn" and "Why Join Us?" sections, alternate lines rapidly. Look at each other slightly to pass the baton, then look right back at the audience to show synergy.
